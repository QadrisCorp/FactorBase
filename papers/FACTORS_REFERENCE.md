## Factor Models Timeline

### Three-Factor Model (1993)
**Paper:** Fama & French (1993)  
**Factors:** Market, Size (SMB), Value (HML)  
**Impact:** Foundation of modern factor investing

### Four-Factor Model (1997)
**Paper:** Carhart (1997)  
**Factors:** Market, Size (SMB), Value (HML), Momentum (UMD)  
**Impact:** Added momentum to capture short-term persistence

### Five-Factor Model (2015)
**Paper:** Fama & French (2015)  
**Factors:** Market, Size, Value (HML), Profitability (RMW), Investment (CMA)  
**Impact:** Extended model with profitability and investment

### q-Factor Model (2015)
**Paper:** Hou, Xue, Zhang (2015)  
**Factors:** Market, Size (ME), Investment (I/A), Profitability (ROE)  
**Impact:** Investment-based factor model with strong explanatory power

### Quality Factor (2019)
**Paper:** Asness, Frazzini, Pedersen (2019)  
**Factors:** Quality (QMJ - Quality Minus Junk)  
**Impact:** Comprehensive quality measure combining multiple characteristics

## Key Measures by Paper

### Fama & French (1993)
- **Market Factor:** Market return minus risk-free rate
- **Size Factor (SMB):** Small Minus Big - market equity
- **Value Factor (HML):** High Minus Low - book-to-market equity

### Carhart (1997)
- **Momentum Factor (UMD):** Up Minus Down - prior 12-month return (excluding most recent month)

### Fama & French (2015)
- **Profitability (RMW):** Robust Minus Weak - operating profitability
- **Investment (CMA):** Conservative Minus Aggressive - asset growth

### Novy-Marx (2013)
- **Gross Profitability:** (Revenue - COGS) / Total Assets

### Hou, Xue, Zhang (2015)
- **Investment (I/A):** Annual change in total assets / lagged total assets
- **Profitability (ROE):** Quarterly earnings / book equity

### Asness et al (2013)
- **Value:** Various measures across asset classes (P/B, P/E, etc.)
- **Momentum:** Prior returns across asset classes

### Asness et al (2019)
- **Quality (QMJ):** Composite of profitability, growth, safety, and payout
